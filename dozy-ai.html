What Is Amazon Bedrock?

Amazon Bedrock is a fully managed service that offers a choice of high-performing foundation models (FMs) from leading AI companies through a single API, along with a broad set of capabilities needed to build generative AI applications with security, privacy, and responsible AI at the forefront.

What Is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) represents a significant leap forward in the field of natural language processing and information retrieval. It addresses some of the key limitations of traditional language models by combining their powerful language generation capabilities with the ability to access and utilize external, up-to-date information—in this case, your organization's proprietary data.

How Our RAG Process Works

  1. Query Processing | When a user inputs a query or prompt, our system first processes this input to understand the information needed.
  2. Secure Retrieval | Based on the processed query, the system searches your organization's knowledge base to find the most relevant information. This search is conducted entirely within your secure data environment, ensuring that no sensitive information leaves your control.
  3. Information Extraction and Pre-processing | Once relevant documents or data points are retrieved, the system extracts the most pertinent information. This extracted information then undergoes pre-processing.
  4. Context Integration | The pre-processed, retrieved information is then seamlessly integrated with the original query, creating an enriched context for the AI to work with.
  5. Generation | The AI model, now armed with both its pre-trained knowledge and the retrieved, context-specific information from your organization, generates a response. This response is typically more accurate, informative, and relevant than what a standard AI model could have produced on its own.
  6. Output Refinement | In our advanced system, there are additional steps to refine the output, such as fact-checking against the retrieved information, adjusting the response style to match your organization's tone, and ensuring compliance with any specific data handling policies you may have.
